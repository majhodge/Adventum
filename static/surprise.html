Tajima Ramen


Welcome to Tajima Convoy. At our original location, our menu features wide variety of Japanese entrees, appetizers, desserts and signature ramen dishes. Drink selections include a rotating list of locally brewed craft beers, imported Japanese craft beers, and premier selection of sake. Our 1,100-sq. foot dining area features a large community table, great for casual dining and meeting new friends, while small private tables allow for a more intimate dining experience. Guests can enjoy lunch, dinner and late night menus offered daily. Stop by and find out why Tajima has the best ramen and Japanese food in town!
